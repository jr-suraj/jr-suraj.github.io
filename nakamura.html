Minimalism Meets Modern Living

The Nakamura House project required a holistic approach to capture the stark, beautiful minimalism of this modern architectural marvel. The goal was to create a visual identity that felt as clean, structured, and deliberate as the building itself.

I developed a stark red geometric logo that mirrors the distinctive rooflines of the property, providing a bold contrast against the predominantly white and timber facades of the home. The photography session focused on natural lighting, emphasizing the sharp angles, the warmth of the wooden slat details, and the serene landscaping surrounding the property.

The visual foundation of Nakamura House relies on a strict, minimalist system. These core guidelines ensure consistency across all architectural branding and marketing touchpoints.
