ZIP-O-LOG Mills

Client Zip-O-Log Mills (USA)
Role Rebranding
Year 2024 (Anniversary)

A Legacy Carved in Timber

Zip-O-Log Mills represents the heavy-duty heart of the American timber industry. To celebrate 80 years of excellence (1944–2024), I was tasked with an anniversary evolution of their original "Zip-O-Log Mills" brand mark.

The goal was to create an identity that felt as grounded and industrial as the logs they process, while introducing a celebratory fluidity through the landscape ribbon. This anniversary mark acts as a bridge between their historical founding and their future-facing sustainability goals.

Anatomy of the Rebrand

01

The "80" Wood Logs

The anniversary numeral is rendered as raw wood log cross-sections. The growth rings symbolize the layers of company history and institutional knowledge built since 1944.

02

Landscape Ribbon

The base ribbon represents the rugged timberlands. Its geometric flow mirrors the topography of the US logging sites, anchoring the logo to the land itself.
